About Sarah

Sarah is a visionary AI developer with over 8 years of experience in machine learning, smart home technology, and sustainable innovation. She holds a Master's degree in Computer Science from MIT and has worked with leading tech companies before becoming an independent creator.

Her passion lies in creating intelligent systems that not only solve complex problems but also contribute to a more sustainable future. Sarah believes that AI should be accessible, ethical, and beneficial for everyone.

When she's not coding, Sarah enjoys hiking, reading science fiction novels, and mentoring young developers. She's also an active advocate for women in tech and frequently speaks at conferences about the future of AI and its impact on society.
