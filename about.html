Intellectual Property Rights in Sierra Leone

Understanding your rights and protection under Sierra Leone law and international frameworks

Sierra Leone IP Laws

Local legal framework

  • Copyright Act 2011: Protects literary, artistic, and musical works for the life of the author plus 70 years
  • Patent Act 2010: Provides 20-year protection for inventions and innovations
  • Trademark Act 2014: Protects brand names, logos, and distinctive marks
  • Industrial Designs Act: Covers aesthetic aspects of functional items

International Treaties

Global protection standards

  • WIPO Member: Sierra Leone is a member of the World Intellectual Property Organization
  • Berne Convention: Automatic copyright protection in 179+ countries
  • Madrid Protocol: International trademark registration system
  • PCT System: Patent Cooperation Treaty for international patents
